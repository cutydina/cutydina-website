Work focused on creating a cartoon for each member of [Gift Oasis](http://thegiftoasis.com) company, based in London, UK. The owner was a big fan of Family Guy, so most of the characters are designed in this style. Many of the employees decided diferent styles and in the end there was a very curious potpourri of styles and characters. And later used to make some holidays and special wallpapers and postal cards.
