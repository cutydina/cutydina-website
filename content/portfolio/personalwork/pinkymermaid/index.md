Generally in May the **#mermay** is celebrated, a social network trend in which during the whole month illustrators draw a mermaid a day during all month, usually done in ink or pencil. I liked the initiative, but due to work amount I couldn't afford to update daily. So I thought instead of doing one a day, I'd do a more detailed illustration for this month. And the truth is, I liked the result, and as you can see, I'm always mixing sensuality with color.
