An interesting story behind this commission. After going to **Tec High School** here in Colima to offer my services as a teacher, and since it was not feasible due some bureaucratic issues, the director of the school really liked my portfolio, so he soon asked me for an illustration for the School. They asked me for a full map of the campus but very colorful and easy to understand. It was tricky because I had to build on their infrastructure and give it some perspective. But at the end, I like the final result.
